This system allows managers to make scheduling decisions based on anticipated customer load. Based on input of number of customers expected in an hour and on how long, on average, it takes a cashier to check out a customer, it calculates data about

This data is applicable to situations where all customers form a single line for multiple registers. Queues are simulated as M/M/c queues, meaning that the model assumes that customer arrivals are a Poisson process, and that service times are exponentially distirbuted. It also assumes that customers are serve in the order they arrive.

Terms:

  • Average Queue Length: The average number of customers in line, excluding those at the register.
  • Average System Time: How long, on average, it takes a customer from entering line to leaving the register.
  • Server Utilization: Percentage of the time a cashier is working.
